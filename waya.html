WAYANAD

Natural lovers and adventure enthusiasts will find Kuruva Island the most suitable space for spending a quality time with their beloved ones or even alone. The Banasura Sagar dam is encircled amidst a beautiful nature with a lot of options to do trekking and similar adventure activities. Hundreds of tourists arrive at the dam to enjoy its beauty and take photographs. It is a part of the Indian Banasura Sagar Project consisting of a dam and a canal project.Major activities here are sightseeing, trekking, boating.Pookode lake is the most beautiful freshwater lake located in South India nestled amidst the large evergreen forests and multitudes of mountains slopes.
