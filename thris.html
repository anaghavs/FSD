THRISSUR

The majesty of Athirappilly waterfalls can be understood by the fact that the falls are located on the Chalakudy River, that originates from the highest mountain in India outside of the Himalayas. A perfect place for a group outing, these falls are sometimes called the Niagra of India.The largest temple in the state dedicated to Goddess Vaishnavi, the Paramekkavu Bhagavathy Temple is one of the most famous places to visit in Thrissur.This almost 1000-year-old temple is a centre of attraction in the time of celebrations in the state.The Kerala Kalamandalam was what gave three main arts of Kerala a new life, namely Kathakali, Kudiyattam, and Mohiniyattam.The best part is that the institute is housed on the banks of the Bharathapuzha River.
