MALAPPURAM

Nedumkayam is home to a wide variety of flora and fauna you can spot elephants, bison, tiger, rabbit, blue monkey, bear, wildcat and deer while you are on a visit here. The forests here are actually a part of the larger Nilgiri Biosphere Reserve. A great attraction here is the wooden rest house in the forest which was built during the colonial era.Keralam Kundu Waterfalls are undoubtedly the best of all Malappuram Tourist Places. Also, it is believed that the water has many medicinal properties.Water scooters,speed boats or idle cruises are attractions here.The main attraction of the Padinjarekara beach is its location, where two rivers join together into the sea.Paramotoring ride at the beach operated by the District Tourism Promotion Council is another attraction here.
